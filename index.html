REveL dataset: RGB-Event-Lidar dataset for assistive robotics

This website will host the dataset introduced in our IEEE CASE 2024 paper named:

Towards Robust Perception for Assistive Robotics: An RGB-Event-LiDAR Dataset and Multi-Modal Detection Pipeline

Adam Scicluna, Cedric Le Gentil, Sheila Sutjipto and Gavin Paul

It will be released by the conference's start on the 28th of August 2024.
